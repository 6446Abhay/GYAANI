Welcome to the official RIE Ajmer Alumni Group! This platform is a dedicated space for the proud alumni of the Regional Institute of Education, Ajmer, to reconnect, collaborate, and celebrate our shared legacy. Whether you're a recent graduate or an experienced professional, this community is here to strengthen the bond we all share with our alma mater.

Our Mission

To bring together RIE Ajmer alumni from across the globe, fostering meaningful connections and supporting professional growth while contributing to the advancement of education and society.

What We Offer

Networking Opportunities: Reconnect with classmates, build professional relationships, and explore collaborative opportunities. Events & Activities: Stay updated on reunions, webinars, workshops, and community programs. Mentorship & Support: Guide current students and younger alumni while learning from experienced peers. Celebrating Success: Share achievements, milestones, and contributions to inspire the community. Join us as we cherish our memories, create new ones, and continue to uphold the values and excellence that RIE Ajmer represents. Together, let’s build a vibrant community that inspires, empowers, and transforms.
